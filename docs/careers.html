Working at 1partCarbon is an exciting and fulfilling experience. Our team is dedicated to creating innovative solutions for our clients, and we’re always pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.

Why work with 1partCarbon

We have a relaxed and supportive work environment, where creativity and collaboration are highly valued. Our team is made up of individuals from diverse backgrounds and varied locations, bringing unique perspectives and skills to the table, making every day a learning opportunity.

At 1partCarbon, we believe in balancing hard work with fun. Our get togethers, social, and sporting activities are a testament to that. If you’re looking for a dynamic and fast-paced work environment where you can make a real impact, 1partCarbon is the place for you.

Software positions

Location: Edinburgh, Amsterdam and/or remote

Salary: Dependent on experience

We are seeking experienced and junior software developers to join our growing team.

Experienced Developers

The ideal candidates will have a background in developing and deploying robust, scalable and secure web applications.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable and high-performance web applications
  • Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and solve complex technical problems
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs
  • Develop and implement security and data protection measures
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends

Requirements:

  • A degree in Computer Science or related field or equivalent professional experience
  • Experience in full stack development
  • Expertise in at least one modern front-end framework (e.g. React, Angular)
  • Strong knowledge of back-end programming languages (e.g. Node.js, Ruby on Rails, Python)
  • Experience with database technologies (e.g. MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL)
  • Understanding of software architecture patterns
  • Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities

We offer a dynamic and challenging work environment, competitive salary, and a comprehensive benefits package. If you are a self-motivated individual with a passion for web development and a desire to work with a talented team, we encourage you to apply.

Please email your CV and a cover letter highlighting your relevant experience and qualifications to hr@1partcarbon.co.uk

Junior Developers

We are also looking to recruit junior developers who have recently graduated with a Computer Science or equivalent degree or have completed an intensive developer course (e.g CodeClan) and are interested in joining, learning and contributing to our team.
