At the start of the Covid pandemic we closed our offices and collaborating with over 50 volunteer engineers and helpers we converted our offices into a PPE factory using loaned 3D printers and part financed by a crowdfunder appeal we supplied around 50,000 3D printed face shields manufactured to EN 166 standard to Scottish hospitals and medical teams.
