1partCarbon offer complete end to end digital services from initial discovery and problem solving, to project planning, through to final implementation.

Our methodology

Our teams are a flexible resource, accelerating the build of beautifully crafted digital products and services while sharing our knowledge of software process and design.

How we can help

We work for or alongside our clients and in dedicated teams, so you benefit from both the knowledge and experience of individual engineers and that of the wider team.

We spike new ideas, build MVPs and prototypes, port legacy systems and build out complete products. Project timescales can vary and some clients keep us on as their out-sourced, ”in-house” development team.

Discovery & problem solving

We work with our customers to resolve problems and provide dynamic solutions. The team is experienced at solving complex problems with elegant solutions, helping the customer get from inception of an idea to something that just works.

User experience

Our design and development process is based around the user needs. We design for the user, test with user feedback during development and iterate your development as appropriate.

Visual design

We create visual concepts, developing overall layout and production design for applications which communicate ideas, inspire, inform, and captivate.

Software Engineering

Our core skillset is computer science and software engineering. Our projectapproach is iterative and Agile and our extensive skill set allows us to choose the right tools for the job.

Mobile app development

We’ve developed and brought to market many apps from native mobile through app stores to apps delivered on the web using the right technology to deliver the best mobile user experience for your product and audience.

Data management & processing

We have considerable experience in designing, processing and optimising large data sets in healthcare, biotechnology, mobile and financial data.

Digital firefighting

Our breadth of experience means we are often called in to help customers get projects back on track.

Systems, penetration testing, & digital security consulting

With an increasing emphasis on data privacy we have found our team’s interest in and our work around ethical hacking has expanded considerably. We have provided these services in the healthcare and government sectors.
